Watemelons: A Comprehensive Analysis

By Gavin Dooley

In this web page, we will investigate the many varieties of watermelon available for consumption. The watermelon is among the most versatile of fruits. Every type of watermelon has its own unique traits. We will discuss texture, flavor and color. Reading this page is about as close as you can come to actually eating a watermelon through your computer screen. Enjoy!

    Of course, I know that eating watermelons at a serious level can make it quite difficult to keep a slim figure.
    Here is some java code that can help you keep track of your daily consumption. 

    String response;
int count;
while (true){
	System.out.println("Did you eat another watermelon? (Type y or n)");
	response = reader.nextLine();
	if (response.equals("y")
		count++;
	else
		break;
}
System.out.println("Not bad! You only ate "+count+" watermelons today! Let's try to get that down to "+count-1+" tommorow.");

    Gaining acceptance in the elite watermeloning community can seem daunting. Here are some basic vocab terms you should know.

    Permeabilty
    A melon's capability to absorb water
    Seedling
    A baby seed
    Shell
    The outer crust of a melon
    Heart
    The rock found at the center of a watermelon
    Salinity
    The salt content of a watermelon
