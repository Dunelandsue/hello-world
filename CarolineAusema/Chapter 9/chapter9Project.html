CHS Musical: CHICAGO High School Edition

The Chesterton High School Music and Drama Departments will present the musical,
"CHICAGO High School Edition" by Fred Ebb, Bob Foss and John Kander on Friday,
November 15 at 7:00 p.m., Saturday, November 16 at 2:00 p.m. and 7:00 p.m. and Sunday,
November 17 at 2:00 p.m. in the CHS Auditorium, 2125 S 11th Street in Chesterton. Ticket
prices are as follows: $10 for Adults, $8 for Students & Seniors. Tickets can be purchased
at the CHS Bookstore from 8 a.m.-Noon weekdays beginning on Monday, October 14.


Parents. . .

Hello - Did you know you can call our Attendance Hotline 24 hours a day to leave a message
regarding your student's absence? Please be sure to contact us as early as possible to avoid having
your child's absence marked as unexcused. When leaving a message, please include your name, the
student's name and spelling of their name, grade, and a phone number where we can reach you if
necessary. The Attendance Hotline number is 219-983-3731.
