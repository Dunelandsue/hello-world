Mars
"Home of the Martians"

Mars is the 2nd smallest planet in the Solar System (excluding Pluto) and the 4th and last of the inner terrestrial planets. It is named after the Roman god of war and often refered to as the "Red Planet". This is due to the presence of iron oxide that is of abundance on the Martian surface. Look below to see the complete makeup of Mars.

  1. Atmosphere
    • 95.97% carbon dioxide
    • 1.93% argon
    • 1.89% nitrogen
    • < 1% Other Gases
  2. Crust
  3. Mantle
  4. Core

Although the popular belief of intelligent "Martians" living on Mars's surface didn't come into effect until the late 19th century, these facts have been in effect for billions of years!
