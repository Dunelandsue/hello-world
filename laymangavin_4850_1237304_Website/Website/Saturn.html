Saturn
"The Ring Bearer"

Saturn is the 2nd largest planet within the Solar System, behind only Jupiter. It gets its name from the Roman god of wealth and agriculture, and is most well known for the impressive rings that orbit around it. Although most of the other gas giants have rings as well, the layers listed below are unique solely to Saturn.

  1. Atmosphere
    • 96.3% hydrogen
    • 3.25% helium
    • < 1% Other Gases
    • Ammonia, water, and ammonium hydrosulfide in ice state.
  2. Hydrogen gas
  3. Liquid helium
  4. Ices
  5. Core
