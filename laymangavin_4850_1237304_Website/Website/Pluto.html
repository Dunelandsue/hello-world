Pluto
"The Outcast"

Pluto is the furthest of the planets prevously mentioned within this website. Pluto is named after the Roman ruler of the underworld. Prevously classified as a planet along with the eight that come before it, it was formally downgraded to a "dwarf" planet in 2006 because of its small size. Below is the makeup of Pluto.

  1. Atmosphere
    • Nitrogen
    • Methane
    • Carbon Monoxide
  2. Ice Mantle
  3. Core

Pluto's orbit is also very unique, as it is so elliptical that it crosses paths with Neptune at 2 seperate points. This allows many chances for the two bodies to either collide or for Pluto to get pulled into Neptune's gravitational pull. The table below shows other Pluto facts.
