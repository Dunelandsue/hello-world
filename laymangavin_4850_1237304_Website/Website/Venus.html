Venus
"Earth's Sister Planet"

Mercury is the second closest planet to the Sun within the Solar System. It is named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty. Venus and Earth are very similar in size, mass proximity, and bulk composition, and can even by seen with the naked eye in broad daylight (rarely). While also being the 2nd brightest object in the night sky behind the Moon. Venus has a fairly similar makeup to Earth regarding its layers as well.

  1. Atmosphere
    • 96.5% Carbon Dioxide
    • 3.5% Nitrogen
    • < 1% Other Gases
  2. Crust
  3. Mantle
  4. Core
