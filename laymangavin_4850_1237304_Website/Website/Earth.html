The Earth
"The Holder of Life"

The Earth is home to all known life in the Solar System, and is the 3rd closest planet to the Sun. It is also the only planet within the confides of the Solar System in which water can be present in all 3 states at the same time: a solid, a liquid, and a gas. The layers or the Earth, if you don't know already, are as follows:

  1. Atmosphere
    • 78.08% Nitrogen
    • 20.95% Oxygen
    • ~1% Water Vapor
    • < 1% Other Gases
  2. Crust
  3. Mantle
  4. Tectonic Plates
  5. Outer Core
  6. Inner Core
